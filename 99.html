About Me

Hi, I'm Emma! I'm in my second year of Strategic Product Design at TU Delft, where I'm learning to design products and services that work for people and for the planet. My interests sit at the crossroads of sustainability consulting, strategic design and graphic design — and I believe good ideas often start with a pen and a piece of paper, which is why I draw a lot.

Outside of class I take on small commissions through Em.maakt — birthday cards, illustrations, the occasional commission. I like working with people who care about what they make. If that sounds like you, get in touch!
